Something to Avoid

Every skier must know things they should avoid. However, there is one thing that drives all skiers crazy and are really annoying. They are snowboarders. The number one thing that skiers must avoid is snowboarders. They bump into skiers and sit in the middle of the slope, too lazy to stand up. When snowboarders sit in the middle of the slope, a skier comes and accidentally hits them. Then they get mad for something that is their fault. When snowboarders bump into people, it is very painful. There were many times when snowboarders broke a skier's bone by bumping into them. Snowboarders even break skiers' ski equipment. These people are just dumb and it is the best to avoid them.
